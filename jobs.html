Opportunities in the lab

We welcome motivated researchers to join us to explore exciting genome sciences together.

Successful applicants will have the opportunities to work on both collaboration projects to study human genetic diseases and projects on developing computational and statistical methods to tackle cutting-edge research questions arise from large-scale genetic and genomic data. Trainees in the lab will receive quality mentorship and training to help achieve their professional goals. In addition to the assigned projects, we also encourage trainees to propose their own ideas under the research theme of the lab.

Competitive salary and benefit package will be provided to successful applicants based on experience.

Postdoctoral Fellow

Requirements:
1. Ph.D. in computer sciences, biostatistics, bioinformatics, genetics, or related fields.
2. Strong quantitative research background and computational skills.
3. Experience on analyzing large-scale genetic/genomic data sets is preferred.
4. Demonstrated excellence in research (e.g., high-quality publications in reputable journals).
5. Good communication skills in English.
How to apply:
Applicants should send their CV, a brief research statement, one representative publication, and arrange 3 reference letters to Chaolong Wang via email.

Research Officer (Data Analyst)

Requirements:
1. Bachelor or Master degree in computer sciences, biostatistics, bioinformatics or related fields.
2. Strong computational and programming skills.
3. Experience on analyzing large-scale genetic/genomic data sets is preferred.
4. Good communication skills in English.
How to apply:
Applicants should send their CV and arrange 2-3 reference letters to Chaolong Wang via email.

Others

Opportunities for graduate students, exchange students, visiting scholars and internship in the lab might be available. Please send your inquiries to Chaolong Wang via email.
