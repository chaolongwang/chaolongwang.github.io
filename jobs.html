Opportunities in the lab

We welcome motivated researchers to join us to explore exciting genome sciences together.

We are part of the Singapore National Precision Medicine Program and the Centre for Big Data and Integrative Genomics (c-BIG). Multiple positions are available for research scientists, postdocs, software engineers, and data analysts. Successful applicants will have the opportunities to collaborate with scientists and clinicians across the nation and work on whole genome sequencing data of >10,000 Singaporeans for population genetics questions and various disease phenotypes, including Electronic Medical Records.

Trainees in the lab will receive quality mentorship and training to help achieve their professional goals. In addition to the assigned projects, we also encourage trainees to propose their own ideas under the research theme of the lab, especially novel methodology research.

Competitive salary and benefit package will be provided to successful applicants based on experience.

How to apply:
Applicants should send their CV and help arrange 2-3 reference letters to Chaolong Wang via email.

Postdoctoral Fellow

Requirements:
1. Ph.D. in computer sciences, biostatistics, bioinformatics, genetics, or related fields.
2. Strong quantitative research background.
3. Strong computational skills (familiar with at least 1 programming languages).
4. Experience on analyzing large-scale genetic/genomic data sets is preferred.
5. Demonstrated excellence in research (at least 1 high-quality publications in reputable journals).
6. Good communication skills in English.

Data Analyst

Requirements:
1. Bachelor or Master degree in computer sciences, biostatistics, bioinformatics or related fields.
2. Strong computational and programming skills.
3. Experience on analyzing large-scale genetic/genomic data sets is preferred.
4. Good communication skills in English.

Software Engineer

Requirements:
1. Bachelor or Master degree in computer sciences, bioinformatics or related fields.
2. Familiar with C++, Java, and (or) Phython.
3. Experience in developing software packges or web-based servers is highly preferred.
4. Good communication skills in English.

Others

Opportunities for graduate students, exchange students, visiting scholars and internship in the lab might be available. Please send your inquiries to Chaolong Wang via email.
